Devenmat | Valantis
In reply to this message
Sure. It's a very configurable construction where each asset has a target reserve ratio, linear fee rate, and peg module.
So say we target the pool as 50% wBTC, 30% cbBTC, 15% dlcBTC, 5% tBTC.

When the pool is overweight dlcBTC, swap fees increase accordingly, tuned for each asset based on risk tolerance, to the incentivize a return to equillibrium. LPs make money as the pool moves around the equillibrium.
Also each asset gets its own custom peg rate module. Imagine dlcBTC trades 1:1 against wBTC when at equillibrium, unless an oracle or watchtower relays a depeg event or the equivalent.
