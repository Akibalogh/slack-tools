Aki Balogh 24.08.2023 10:08:37
One tweak:

Once BTC has been locked in a DLC, our system sends a "DLC_funded" event, and your protocol issues the rDlcBTC deposit token. R is minted against it.

If the position is liquidated, your protocol sends a liquidation event to our Attestors. They check it on-chain, and release a signal that causes the BTC to be liquidated and moved to the counterparty (the Router).

Your liquidator pulls from a USDC or USDT pool (and the Router sells BTC to refill that pool). The liquidator sells it for R.
05:00
We ourselves don't have a token, and we're not a bridge. We're a layer that lets protocols implement native Bitcoin support using DLCs
