🗓 Date & Time: January 26, 2024, 3 PM GMT

1、The Evolution of Asset Type Protocols:
"In the Bitcoin ecosystem, we've witnessed the emergence and evolution of various asset-type protocols. How do you view the unique advantages and challenges of developing asset protocols on the Bitcoin blockchain compared to other public blockchains? 2、The Development of Performance Expansion Protocols:
"In recent years, the Bitcoin network has seen various attempts to extend its performance through solutions like Layer 2 and sidechain technologies. What potential do you see in these technologies to enhance the performance of the Bitcoin network? 3、Community-Driven Development of Inscription-Type Projects:
"Inscription-type Bitcoin projects often rely more on community-driven efforts than traditional 'project teams.' 4、The Development Path of the BTC Ecosystem:
