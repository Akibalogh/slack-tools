Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

DLC.Link utilizes Discreet Log Contracts, invented at MIT by the co-creator of the Lightning Network, to provide a trustless bridge to DeFi on Ethereum.

We are launching dlcBTC, a safer wrapped Bitcoin, on Arbitrum in April 2024.
02:26
Unlike WBTC and tBTC, dlcBTC doesn't rely on a third-party custodian or bridge. Instead, it uses Discreet Log Contracts (DLCs), invented at MIT by the co-creator of the Lightning Network. These contracts let depositors "self-wrap" by locking their BTC in a multisig. This ensures that dlcBTC is secure against theft; in any hacking scenario, the BTC can only be returned to the original owner's wallet.

Here's an article on us: https://blockworks.co/news/building-better-wrapped-bitcoin-for-defi
