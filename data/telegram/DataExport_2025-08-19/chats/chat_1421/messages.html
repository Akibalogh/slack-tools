12:53
Aki Balogh
Hi @nach211 - great points made.

Since BTC cannot be staked, there are two ways to earn staking yield on it:
1. Launch a POS chain. Ask users to bridge into the chain with BTC. Downside is, this introduces bridge risk — the BTC can be stolen
2. Use BTC as collateral to secure other chains. This is what Babylon does with native BTC. However, they are limited to their strategies (e.g. Cosmos chains).

Using dlcBTC enables a Babylon-like staking offering using any protocol on any chain. We leverage self-custody on the BTC side and composability on the Eth side
