We make dlcBTC: https://blockworks.co/news/building-better-wrapped-bitcoin-for-defi
dlcBTC, a self-custody, decentralized ,self- wrapped Bitcoin. Unlike WBTC and tBTC, dlcBTC doesn't rely on a third-party custodian or bridge. Instead, it uses Discreet Log Contracts (DLCs), developed at MIT by the creator of the Lightning network, which let depositors "self-wrap" by locking their BTC in a multisig. This ensures that dlcBTC is secure against theft; in any hacking scenario, the BTC can only be returned to the original owner's wallet.
