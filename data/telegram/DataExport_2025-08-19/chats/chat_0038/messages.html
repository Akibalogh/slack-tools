Just to answer one question you had: does dlcBTC use HTLCs? I wanted to add some technical color:

Instead of HTLCs, we actually use a more advanced technology called PTLCs. These are standard in DLCs.

To characterize it: dlcBTC uses DLCs (on Bitcoin L1) to lock BTC into a multisig that can only pay out to the original depositor. Thus, the BTC is kept in self-custody throughout.
