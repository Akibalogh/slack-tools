In short, Stacks has a wrapped Bitcoin called sBTC that lets Bitcoin be used in Stacks applications. SBTC is secured by a network of Stacks validators.

In this regard, they’re similar to Avalanche’s BTCB which lets Bitcoin be wrapped and used in Avalanche apps.

The Bitcoin is locked in a multisig on Bitcoin chain, from where it can be controlled by a Solidity contract. We call this “self-wrapping” since the user doesn’t need another entity
