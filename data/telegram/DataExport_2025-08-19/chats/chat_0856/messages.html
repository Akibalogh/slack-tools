Hi @morgancoincover ‚Äî Happy Monday! Over the weekend, I realized, I've mis-stated scope a bit.

To mint dlcBTC, depositors need to have both a BTC wallet and an Eth wallet. We want to insure both.
14:34
If they lose their BTC private key, that's bad for them individually but it doesn't hurt the system. They could still redeem dlcBTC. Arguably though, they might refuse to to, since they would not receive the BTC and would not have tangible benefit from burning.
14:35
If they lose the ETH private key, that's bad for the entire system. They become categorically unable to redeem, which means that dlcBTC loses some of its liquidity.
14:35
So, we should require merchants to insure & have backups for both wallets they want to use
14:36
If they mint to multiple chains (e.g. Solana), they should have backups on those wallets also
