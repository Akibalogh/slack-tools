Depends on the type of engagement whether it's project-based work or staff augmentation
For staff augmentation we usually work on monthly retainer basis, which means that you pay us a fixed monthly cost each month for each engineer engaged to your team. Here is a rate card with approximate rates for monthly retainers

For project based work we do have a few options - the most common is T&M approach where we bill on hourly basis each 2 weeks (each sprint) for the work done/time spent during the sprint. Also we can work on the fixed price type too for small projects and in this case we just divide a total amount proportionally according to the project timeline. For bigger projects we keep using T&M as it's more cost-efficient, flexible and transparent for customer's side.

Also in the beginning of our cooperation we ask for a deposit payment which is a guarantee payment and will be used to cover the last sprint/month of our cooperation. The amount of the deposit is usually equal for 1 sprint for T&M approach or for 1 monthly retainer for staff augmentation
