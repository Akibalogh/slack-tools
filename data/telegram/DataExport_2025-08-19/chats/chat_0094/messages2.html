Proposed Fees
‚Ä¢ Mint/Burn: 5 bps
‚Ä¢ Transfer: 3 bps
‚Ä¢ Lock/Unlock: 3 bps

1. Should we charge a transfer fee?

Yes. Transfer requires lock/unlock to encumber the CBTC.

Yes. Three use cases for locks:
A. Lock an asset for transfer
B. Pledging collateral (without a transfer), e.g. a loan
C. Delivery vs. payment transaction

We should charge transfer at the same rate as lock/unlock.
