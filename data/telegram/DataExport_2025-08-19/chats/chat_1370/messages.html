Here are the essential wallet requirements for our integration:

* Supports Taproot Addresses

* Able to Sign PSBTs

* Recognize and Sign 2-of-2 Multisig Inputs
* The wallet must either:
* Automatically recognize that the user is a participant in a 2-of-2 multisig input and sign it accordingly
* *OR*
* Allow the creation or registration of a new wallet using a provided 2-of-2 multisig descriptor and sign transactions based on that descriptor
