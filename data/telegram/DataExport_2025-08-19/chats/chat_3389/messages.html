Aki Balogh
Hey Blaize ‚Äî great news! Looks like we've solved the liquidation issue. We've figured out a way to close the DLC and not need an MTL
10:20
I'll describe it here and feel free to ask questions, or I can also walk through it live
10:20
With a DLC, you have Alice and Bob. Bob needs to have a Bitcoin wallet and pre-sign transactions
10:20
The DLC community has always assumed that Bob would receive funds in a liqudation
10:21
But that doesn't have to be the case. There can be a third address that liquidated funds are sent to
10:21
So, in our new understanding:

Alice is the user, Bob is our router wallet that signs for transactions and is an administrator to the loan, and there's a third address to an OTC desk (e.g. FalconX) where liquidated BTC is sent to
10:23
The DeFi protocol can supply this address to us. And we never receive BTC, therefore no need for an MTL
10:23
The DeFi protocol just has to pick an OTC desk that they trust. They need to have a BTC account there, and they need to run their regular liquidation process from there
10:24
The DeFi protocol could either liquidate BTC directly, or could have the OTC desk swap BTC for another token and auction that off
