Overview

Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

DLC.Link utilizes Discreet Log Contracts, invented at MIT by the co-creator of the Lightning Network, to provide a theft-proof bridge to DeFi on Ethereum. 

We've recently launched dlcBTC, a safer wrapped Bitcoin. dlcBTC is the first native wrapped Bitcoin on Arbitrum, Base and XRPL.

We're raised $3Mn to date from OKX, ABCDE, Foresight, Chainlink, Sequoia Scout and 15 other funds, and are raising $5Mn this summer to expand sales & marketing.
