Hey everyone,
I've been doing some research on Saylor's model with STRK, STRF, and MSTR, and it's essentially a copy of the Money on Chain protocol — the only real difference is that the BPRO-equivalent pays interest to the DOC-equivalent. The core idea is the same: splitting BTC exposure and risk across multiple instruments.

The good news is: we can implement exactly this model (or any variation we want) using the current MoC protocol. It would just require adding some constraints on top. As long as the model involves risk distribution between two or more instruments, we can build it — and we can do it in less than 3 months.

Let’s talk if you want to explore launching this or adapting it to something custom.
