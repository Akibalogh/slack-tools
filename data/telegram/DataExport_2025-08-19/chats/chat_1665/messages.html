* Stackswap - exchange on Stacks - add native BTC support
* ALEX - exchange on Stacks - add native BTC support, potential lending capability

Early Interest:
* Circle - two potential use cases: BTC-backed stablecoin and adding native BTC support to CircleYield

* NYDIG - potential lending and trading to support Marathon Digital (miner)
