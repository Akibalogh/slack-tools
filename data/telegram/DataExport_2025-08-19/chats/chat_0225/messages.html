iBTC and 1-Click Yield (1CY)

Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

iBTC is a safer wrapped Bitcoin that uses the Bitcoin Layer 1 network to natively secure deposits. We use Discreet Log Contracts, invented at MIT by the creator of the Lightning Network, to provide a theft-proof BTC bridge to DeFi.

We're also developing 1-Click Yield (1CY), the first native BTC staking for EVM. Unlike Babylon, 1CY is composable with any vault, restaking protocol or DeFi strategy.

We're raised $3Mn to date from OKX, ABCDE, Foresight, Chainlink, Sequoia Scout and 15 other funds, and are now completing a $5Mn seed raise co-led by CMT, with participation from Auros, XBTO, Maelstrom, IMC, Ripple, SamsungNext and others.

dlcBTC, a self-custody, decentralized ,self- wrapped Bitcoin.
Unlike WBTC and tBTC, dlcBTC doesn't rely on a third-party custodian or bridge.
Instead, it uses Discreet Log Contracts (DLCs), developed at MIT by the creator of the Lightning network, which let depositors "self-wrap" by locking their BTC in a multisig.
This ensures that dlcBTC is secure against theft; in any hacking scenario, the BTC can only be returned to the original owner's wallet.
