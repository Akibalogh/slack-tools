Hey Tyler! A couple of comments:

Just to be precise - nobody deposits 100 BTC with DLC.link. You use your software to self-wrap. All 100 BTC stay with you, in a locked state.

If our merchants make a DLC with 100 BTC, and redeem 50 BTC by burning 50 dlcBTC, then they are left with 50 BTC and correspondingly 50 dlcBTC.
