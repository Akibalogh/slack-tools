Overview
Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

DLC.Link utilizes Discreet Log Contracts, invented at MIT by the co-creator of the Lightning Network, to provide a trustless bridge to DeFi on Ethereum.

We are launching dlcBTC, a safer wrapped Bitcoin, on Arbitrum in April 2024.
