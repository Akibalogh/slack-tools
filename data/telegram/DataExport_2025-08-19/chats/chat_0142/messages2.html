The issues/risks are that, in order to do this, there is a lot of new technology that has to be invented. Their whitepaper is quick to point out that there are a lot of missing features that makes it very difficult or impossible to implement certain features. It's a very interesting early-stage idea, and if I were a pre-seed investor, I'd be compelled to invest, as a high-risk high-reward play.
