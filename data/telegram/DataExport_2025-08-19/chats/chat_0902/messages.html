Regarding the multisig wallet, let me add:

One key is held by the depositor and the other by a threshold of Attestors. The DLC mechanism ensures that when the depositor makes a deposit, she pre-signs her wallet as the only conditional outcome. This occurs on BTC L1 and is enforced by the entire Bitcoin network hashrate. This makes the bridge protocol theft-proof: Attestors cannot steal the BTC as they cannot receive it.
