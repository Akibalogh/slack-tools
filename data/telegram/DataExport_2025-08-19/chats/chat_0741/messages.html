Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

iBTC Overview
Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

iBTC is a safer wrapped Bitcoin that uses the Bitcoin Layer 1 network to natively secure deposits. This technology, called “self-wrapping,” employs a 2-of-2 multisig between the user and iBTC’s relay nodes, called attestors. Self-wrapping ensures security while enabling Bitcoin-holding LPs to generate yield through trading activities.
