Here's the refined workflow for minting dlcBTC.

Follow these steps, and your clients can keep their BTC securely in their Ledger wallets while minting dlcBTC using either a single key OR a multisig key setup (if Anton is involved in the signing process).

In other words, Anton acts as an "agent", facilitating the minting process on our platform while allowing your clients to retain full control over their BTC and private keys.

Minting Workflow:

1. Merchant Onboarding:
Anton becomes an official merchant.

2. Whitelisting:
Client A provides their MetaMask EVM address to Anton, which is then whitelisted by dlcBTC. This EVM address can be a multisig (such as MetaMask Institutional) or a single-key wallet.

3. Connecting and Minting:
The client connects their Ledger and mints dlcBTC using the whitelisted EVM address. If the whitelisted address is a multisig, both Client A and Anton will mint together.

4. BTC Locking:
Client A locks their BTC within their Ledger.

5. Receiving dlcBTC:
Client A receives the minted dlcBTC directly into their wallet address.
