I can add a little color to the token conversation here. Chainlink's token distribution was:

40% circulating supply (that includes tokens allocated to investors, plus tokens sold to the public for the ICO),

Prior to the ICO, they sold 5% to investors/advisors, so that's the 40% circulating supply at ICO.

For us, the investment landscape is different, so I'm suggesting a tweak to the size of the pre-market sale vs. ICO: increasing the pre-market amount from 5% up to 15%. Circulating supply would still match CL's 40%, though, because we would likely sell 5% from the Treasury and airdrop 5% from the Ecosystem pool.

I'm using a bit different categories and splitting investments into different types here, but it's basically the same:

(1) ICO Pre-sale investors and Advisors - 15% (4 year vest)
(2) Core team - 15% (4 year vest)
(3) Treasury - 20% (future team, runway investments)
(4) Foundation - 10% (grants, social good investments)
(5) Ecosystem - Staking rewards, Node operators, partners, ecosystem investments - 35%
(6) Airdops - 5%

Where what Chainlink calls "Circulating supply" is (1) + (2) + 5% of (3) + (6).
"Dev Fund" is (3) - 5% that we sell in ICO + (4).
Network Incentive Fund is the same as (5).
