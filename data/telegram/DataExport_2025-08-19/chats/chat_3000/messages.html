Lambda Protocol enables Smart Contracts and DeFi applications natively on Bitcoin’s Layer 1.

🐑 What is Lambda Protocol enabling 🐑
- Trading on AMM
- Layer 2s
- Having native Bitcoin integration
- Borrowing against Bitcoin
- Multifunctional NFTs
- Creating Tokens
- Earning native yield
- ... anything else that’s possible on an EVM chain

🗝 Key Highlights 🗝
🚀 We successfully completed our Initial DEX Offering (IDO) for our LRC-20 token $LMDA, representing 10% of the total token supply, for almost 10 BTC.
🌐 We're currently in the open beta testing phase on the mainnet, allowing users to experience the platform firsthand via https://apps.lambdaprotocol.io/. Liquidity is currently capped to ensure user safety.
🔧 Our platform features a suite of working tools, including DMT (deploy/mint/transfer tokens), OTC (buy/sell tokens with others), NFT (mint your own NFT/JPG), Kitchen (stake your tokens and earn other tokens), Explorer, and more.
💡 We have an upcoming hackathon with exciting prizes to onboard developers and foster innovation within the ecosystem.
🤝 Our main goal is to provide the foundational infrastructure for other projects to build upon and innovate, fostering a vibrant ecosystem of decentralized applications and services. Therefore we aim to collaborate, not compete. We strive to synergize with other protocols/projects and support them in maximizing the potential of their projects/protocols through smart contracts!
