Angus Lamps | Stargate Foundation LayerZero
So Stargate operates as a DAO, and there are currently two typical methods of getting a token listed on Stargate

1) Governance proposal for an Autonomous Pool Deployment. You would draft up a proposal that specifies which chains you'd need dlcBTC on, and then supply your own liquidity into these pools, allowing your users to bridge between chains.

2) Any tokens that employ LayerZero's Omnichain Fungible Token standard (OFT) can be listed immediately, as there is a DAO mandate to do so, and is a very easy technical lift. If you're looking to have dlcBTC available on multiple chains, OFT is by far the easiest and most secure approach. I've added @lzBill from who leads LayerZero's DeFi team in case you had questions on the OFT standard
