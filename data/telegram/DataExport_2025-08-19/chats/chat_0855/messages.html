Aki Balogh
Hi @krantix ! Big news from our end. Over the holidays, we achieved a significant breakthrough in overcoming a major technical challenge.

Previously, Bitcoin wallets needed to integrate our DLC signing library and an adaptor signatures library (e.g. the one developed by Blockstream) to use DLCs. However, the lack of adaptor signature support in BTC wallets made this a substantial integration effort.

Jesse and Nadav discovered a solution by redesigning DLC.Link to utilize PSBTs instead of adaptor signatures. Most wallets already support PSBTs, since the feature is frequently used in Ordinals trading. For example, OKX Wallet can now support DLC.Link.

We are now restructuring our entire codebase to operate on PSBTs. This is a departure from the theoretical concept of a DLC, but it removes our technical barriers while preserving our fundamental value proposition of self-wrapping/self-locking. As before, our attestor network is unable to steal BTC funds.
