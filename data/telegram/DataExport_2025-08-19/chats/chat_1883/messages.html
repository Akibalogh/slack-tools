iBTC Overview
Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

iBTC is a safer wrapped Bitcoin that uses the Bitcoin Layer 1 network to natively secure deposits. We use Discreet Log Contracts, invented at MIT by the creator of the Lightning Network, to provide a theft-proof BTC bridge to DeFi.

We're also developing 1-Click Yield (1CY), the first native BTC staking for EVM. Unlike Babylon, 1CY is composable with any vault, restaking protocol or DeFi strategy.

We're raised $3Mn to date from OKX, ABCDE, Foresight, Chainlink, Sequoia Scout and 15 other funds, and are now raising a $5Mn seed round. Seed round investors include Cumberland, Auros Ventures, Maelstrom, Round 13 Digital and others as well as angel participation from Paul Taylor (BlackRock), Mayur Gupta (CMO Kraken), David Shin (Tezos), Matt Paik (LayerZero) and Charles Mountain (Ether.fi).
