10:46
Aki Balogh
In reply to this message
It would be up to the individual miners/holders.

Joe had mentioned, Foundry leadership was discussing the opportunity for miners to use a variety of CeFi and DeFi lenders. These could be accessible through a tab within Foundry software.

In the case of dlcBTC, the miners could choose to acquire dlcBTC (through minting or swapping), and use it in AAVE. Of course, other DeFi lenders are possible also.

And if the holders want to stake it in an LRT or use it otherwise in DeFi, of course they could do that instead.
