Quick comments:

The paper dates one year back, and it describes a theoretical approach. What we do has been theorized, developed and tested over several years (starting with Suredbits' pioneering work 2019 onward). Also: the WE system sounds like an enhancement to Avalanche's BTC.B. There, 8 wardens in Intel SGX enclaves secure the system. 6-of-8 is required to verify a transaction. In the WE paper, they propose an N-of-N to secure the system.
