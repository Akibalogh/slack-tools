CrossCurve x dlcBTC partnership proposal

About CrossCurve:

CrossCurve enables low-slippage cross-chain swaps of any assets (LSTs, Stables, Curve LPs) in one click, utilizing Curve's deep liquidity pools ($2B+ TVL). Powered by Eywa, Curve’s official cross-chain data aggregation protocol: no more reliance on a single bridge when moving large liquidity positions (Curve's tweet).

Proposal:

We propose to create a unified liquidity market for dlcBTC using CrossCurve’s tools, which will allow the conversion of various versions of Bitcoin (WBTC, tBTC, BTC.b, cbBTC) on the eight largest EVM blockchains into dlcBTC using a single liquidity pool. Additionally, we plan to offer support for Solana, TON, and Tron in the near future.

Supported Chains: Ethereum, Polygon, BNB Chain, Arbitrum, Base, Fantom, Avalanche, Optimism, Gnosis Chain, Blast, Mantle, Taiko, Linea.

Overview
In the meeting titled "dlcBTC | CrossCurve and Jesse Eisenberg," Jesse Eisenberg, CTO and co-founder of a Bitcoin utility-focused company, presented their non-custodial bridge solution for Bitcoin, which allows users to lock Bitcoin and receive a wrapped ERC-20 token. Boris from CrossCurve introduced their cross-chain messaging solution designed to enhance security and liquidity across decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms by combining multiple protocols and offering a unique DEX architecture. The discussion shifted towards potential collaboration, with Boris suggesting a memorandum of understanding, which Jesse expressed interest in after consulting with his co-founder. CrossCurve offered to share their technology and audit findings to facilitate this partnership, focusing on enhancing the liquidity of dlcBTC across various pools. Action items included further research on CrossCurve's solutions and potential agreements to support an upcoming token launch.
Notes

🚀 Introduction and Product Overview
Jesse introduced himself as CTO and co-founder of a company focused on making Bitcoin useful in DeFi
Their product is a non-custodial/self-custodial bridge for Bitcoin
Users lock Bitcoin in a 2-of-2 multisig, with the user retaining one signature
The bridge issues an ERC-20 wrapped token on the other side
They're adding Layer Zero support and already live on Chainlink CCIP
Planning to onboard more TVL in the next couple of months

💼 CrossCurve's Value Proposition
Boris explained CrossCurve's solution for insecure bridge communications
CrossCurve combines different independent cross-chain messaging protocols
They aim to prevent hacks like the multi-chain hack
CrossCurve solves the problem of fragmented liquidity
They offer a cross-chain DEX with a unique architecture using a middle chain

🔄 CrossCurve's Functionality
CrossCurve aggregates different synthetic assets backed by original assets
They combine assets in a hub chain (currently Phantom, soon Sonic) in a stable swap pool
Users can swap different versions of Bitcoin between chains in one place
CrossCurve supports different OFT standards and can create pools for omni-chain tokens
They provide constant incentives for clients through tokenomics
Curve's CEO is a lead investor in CrossCurve
Curve is implementing CrossCurve's bridge (Xdao Bridge) for fast transfers

🤝 Potential Collaboration
