Here's a high level overview of the application currently being built on-chain, that I can absolutely see your team taking advantage of:

Digital Asset is developing an on-chain collateral and margin management solution for bilateral derivatives trading on the Canton network, in collaboration with market makers and the CFTC (there will be a press release announding design partners on Wednesday).

The solution aims to address collateralization inefficiencies by offering real-time margining, increased collateral mobility, transaction privacy, and support for tokenized real-world asset collateral. It uses the Canton ledger for a single source of truth between trading counterparties, enabling real-time updates and automation.

Key features include on-chain capture of collateral agreements (i.e.: ISDA / CSA), workflow execution, automatic collateral checks, and instantaneous settlement of tokenized collateral, with pledged collateral remaining private to the parties involved.

Once ready, the application will be open for any derivs trading desk interested in bringing the risk / collateral management of their trades on chain (you all included, in case you'd like to trade with your clients or market makers on-chain).
