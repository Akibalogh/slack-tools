Bitcoin is the largest digital asset class. But, over $100Bn has been lost in custodian failures and bridge hacks.

The issue is, using third-party custody or a bridge adds counterparty risk. For institutions, this is a major risk. It’s not your keys, not your Bitcoin.

IBTC is an institutional wrapped Bitcoin. Depositors mint by locking BTC into a 2-of-2 multisig with our attestor network.

We call this process self-wrapping. Unlike a bridge, custody of the BTC does not transfer. The principal remains with the depositor.

We sit between liquidity providers and trading firms, and provide a compliant asset that can service large players.

We work with asset managers, market makers and exchanges.

And we generate revenues by charging mint/burn fees as well as referral commissions.

We are the wrapped Bitcoin providers for Canton and Ripple. In the future, we will be developing tokenized yield products as well.
