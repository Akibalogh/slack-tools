Project Serenity is a next generation DeFi protocol that enables crypto miners to transform BTC generated by their rigs into stable, predictable USD-denominated cash flows that are finance-able, which will enable the overall industry to grow. This is accomplished by hedging the three main risk components of: (1) power price volatility, (2) BTC price volatility (which is hedged without using derivatives), and (3) hash rate growth, which is addressed by using Project Serenity's novel, defensible, first mover, BTC-denominated DeFi hash rate swaps market where the settlement index is calculated exogenously through an oracle.
