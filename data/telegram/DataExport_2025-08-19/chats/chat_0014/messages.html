Aggregating Wrapper and SatsBridge are two components of a solution designed to unify and solidify Bitcoin liquidity across EVM L2s.

Aggregating Wrapper is a decentralized Bitcoin wrapper that aggregates BTC liquidity from legacy and innovative wrappers across layers, providing a unified ticker governed by DAO of liquidity stakeholders (wrapper vendors, lending protocols, LPs).

SatsBridge is a non-custodial bridging protocol based on Bitcoin Lightning real-time settlement rails. Ultimately it allows every LP to run their own bridge without the need to trust or participate in economically unscalable MPC-federations of a single vendor or custodian, and yielding on lightning instead. The bridge is also immediately pluggable to all major exchanges thanks to Lightning channels, no listing needed.

Together, SatsBridge and Aggregating Wrapper bring circularity to Bitcoin liquidity flows, mitigate vendor gatekeeping risks and bridging dependency. Ultimately setup makes Bitcoin more liquid, fungible, and redeemable than any underlying solution alone possible. They also reduce costs of the risk reassessment for each wrapper on every layer for lending protocols.
