Jack | Beefy
In reply to this message
Trading Fee APR is generated by the concentrated liquidity position that Beefy creates and manages. Users swapping through the pool pay trading fees to swap through the pool, which Beefy claims and compounds for users of Beefy's CLM product

Vault APR is generated where there are additional liquidity incentives being paid out on top of the LP, which Beefy automatically claims, sells and redeposits. This creates a compounding effect, turning APR into APY.

Boost APR is when Beefy hosts a Boost on a product, where additional incentives are being paid out on the product but not compounded by the vault. In this case, it's the Arbitrum LTIPP incentives which were granted to Beefy and run til 2nd Sept.
