Nucleus is a protocol enabling yield by default (similar to the concept of native yield from the Blast L2) for ETH, BTC, and USD assets. We're currently working with a cohort of 25+ rollups and app-chains (including Movement, Scroll, Sei, Eclipse, Swell, and more) to bring yield by default to their ecosystems.

Our core focus is enable users to earn yield through the infrastructure that rollups and app-chains integrate into their ecosystems. From powering solvers in the chain abstraction ecosystem to building bespoke infra on top of restaking platforms that enable AVSs to acquire rollups as customers more easily, we want users to profit from the activity they create in the networks they participate in in a risk minimized manner.
