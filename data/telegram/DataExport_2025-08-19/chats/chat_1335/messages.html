DLC.Link implements Discreet Log Contracts, an MIT invention that presents the only way to trustlessly move Bitcoin. It was originally proposed in 2018 by Tadge Dryja, the co-inventor of the Lightning Network. They were enabled with Taproot and do not require sidechains, roll-ups or L2s. Instead, DLCs are secured by the hashrate of the entire Bitcoin network.
