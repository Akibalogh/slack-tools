Aki Balogh
Hi Jeffrey! Thank for your excellent questions.

We do have an unofficial trademark for Bitcoin oracles (evidence of first use). With funding, we’ll apply for that and also for provisional patents.

The more oracles we support, the better. For example, at Stacks we work with RedStone. At Solana it would be Pyth. Since the price feed oracle lives on the DeFi app side, we can work with any of them.

The app does all of the calculations and sends a signal to our oracle instructing then to close the escrow with a given outcome. So for example, if the user chooses to trust AAVE, they implicitly choose to trust AAVE’s determination that they’ve defaulted. That is the solution to the “oracle problem” for us — the user has explicitly chosen a DeFi app to trust.
09:45
The rev share with Chainlink — we actually have a call with their head of strategic partnerships (Oliver Birch) and their head of channel partnerships next Tues to discuss. It could be a simple commission, ie they’ll take a fee on revenue generated from partnerships that they initiate.

For example, Chainlink works very closely with AAVE and Compound. We’ll be getting access through them. So they could get paid for offering our service as a cross-sell.

Which would be really cool! Frankly, Chainlink wants to be the global market leader in all oracles, so our Bitcoin oracles are strategic to them. I could see them acquiring us at some point.
