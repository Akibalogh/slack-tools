Hey, I thought you‚Äôd be interested to learn about Flash Liquidity, an exciting new product coming to Radix:

- Flash Liquidity unifies liquidity across the entire crypto ecosystem onto Radix, enabling seamless, capital-efficient trading without liquidity providers needing to pre-lock assets.
- ‚ÄçIt offers traders deep liquidity and low slippage while giving developers access to global crypto liquidity, all within a decentralized framework.

In DeFi, liquidity is scattered across different blockchains and platforms, often making token liquidity network-specific or even DEX-specific. Furthermore, as AMMs require users to pre-lock assets in liquidity pools, this can be capital inefficient and can expose LPs to impermanent loss. As a result, even after failures like FTX, CEXes continue to dominate by offering a more unified trading experience with deeper liquidity.

Flash Liquidity solves this by offering pricing, spreads, and slippage that is comparable to the best centralized venues for the top 50 assets wrapped onto Radix (BTC, ETH, SOL, USDC/T, etc). It works by allowing selected market makers (MMs) the ability to express liquidity offers for these assets without needing to hold the underlying asset. If the liquidity offer is taken (e.g. by a swap coming through a Radix DEX, or a liquidation coming from a CDP or perps exchange), the resulting swap is automatically executed on the Radix Ledger by instantly minting the wrapped asset on demand. The MM then settles the underlying asset a short time later. MMs therefore only provide liquidity when needed, and liquidity is sourced just-in-time for confirmed trades. There‚Äôs no locked capital and no unnecessary price risks.

With Flash Liquidity, traders, dApps and developers in Radix can access the liquidity of the major venues with the speed and efficiency of centralized platforms and the security and transparency of decentralized finance, solving liquidity fragmentation without sacrificing the core principles of DeFi.

G-20, Keyrock, and Portofino have been announced as a trio of launch market makers.

- Technical deep dive: https://www.radixdlt.com/blog/technical-deep-dive-how-flash-liquidity-will-work
