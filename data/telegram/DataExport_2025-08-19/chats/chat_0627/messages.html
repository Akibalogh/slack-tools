Aki Balogh
1. Safer: dlcBTC is minted by institutions ("merchants") from self-custody, by "self-wrapping". No reliance on BitGo or another central point of failure.

2. Ease of Onboarding: It's very easy to onboard as a merchant. The institution must complete KYB (via Fractal ID) and sign a software agreement. Compliance impact is minimal, since there is no change in custody. This enables dlcBTC to be utilized in highly complex compliance environments (E.g. Ripple).

3. Faster: Minting/burning dlcBTC is 3-10X faster than wBTC or tBTC. Current mint/burn is 1 hour (6 Bitcoin block confirmations). In the future, this could even be lowered through product enhancements.

4. Broadly compatible: Since the BTC is locked on the BTC side, it's trivially easy for us to launch on a new chain. If Dialectic wants to exploit yields on a new emerging L2, we can launch there within a few hours.

5. Lower Fees: Our mint/burn fees are 25% cheaper than wBTC. Volume discounts can be negotiated as well.

6. Not a Bridge: Unlike tBTC or other options, we are not a Bitcoin bridge or L2. Institutions have told us that their risk teams will not let them use bridges. We don't have that issue.

7. Existing Institutional Relationships: We're currently onboarding dlcBTC to ~20 institutions, with over 100 more in our pipeline. We can present Dialectic's products to their prop trading teams
