Hey @szx8650 and @Ming516 I hope you guys are doing well! I have an update to share with you.

https://twitter.com/DLC_Link/status/1626192635437191168

We’re locking Bitcoin in what’s called a DLC natively on the Bitcoin chain, minting an NFT representing this, and automatically unlocking this reserved capital when the NFT is burned. Based on who burns the NFT, the BTC goes back to the original spender or some other protocol (lending platform, etc). We have interest in this product for NFT lending platforms, for example.

Now we’re putting some of the contract info into the NFT metadata on IPFS, but we see an opportunity for an SFT to be a better way going forward, keeping more of the information on-chain in a standardized way.

Take a look, let us know what you think. Perhaps we can sync up at some point to see if this usecase is applicable to SFTs
