Here are more details about what I'm working on. I am happy to answer any questions you and your colleagues will have.

We're in the process of launching a new wrapped Bitcoin, dlcBTC. Compared to WBTC and tBTC, dlcBTC doesn't rely on a third-party custodian or bridge. Instead, it uses Discreet Log Contracts (DLCs), developed by MIT, which let depositors "self-wrap" by locking their BTC in a multisig. This ensures that dlcBTC is secure against theft; in any hacking scenario, the BTC can only be returned to the original owner's wallet.
