We have launched a new wrapped Bitcoin called dlcBTC. Unlike WBTC and tBTC, dlcBTC doesn't rely on a third-party custodian or bridge. Instead, it uses Discreet Log Contracts (DLCs), invented at MIT by the co-creator of the Lightning Network. These contracts let depositors "self-wrap" by locking their BTC in a multisig. This ensures that dlcBTC is secure against theft; in any hacking scenario, the BTC can only be returned to the original owner's wallet.

Blockwork article: https://blockworks.co/news/building-better-wrapped-bitcoin-for-defi
