03:37
Bitcoin has proven itself as a store of value. But to take a loan, users have to first send their decentralized Bitcoin to a centralized entity. This is extremely risky, as over $140Bn has been lost due to bridge hacks and custodial failures.

The DLC.link platform lets users lock their Bitcoin in decentralized escrow, directly on Bitcoin chain. Escrow lets the user take loans on Lightning, Ethereum or in any smart contract protocol without needing to send their Bitcoin to a custodian.

Today, less than 2% of Bitcoin is utilized in finance. Escrow will unlock the remaining 98%, enabling Trillions of dollars in future Bitcoin liquidity to be used on any chain.
