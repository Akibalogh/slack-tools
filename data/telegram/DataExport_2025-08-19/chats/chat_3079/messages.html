Unlike wBTC, which is custodied by BitGo, or tBTC, which requires BTC to be send to a bridge, dlcBTC is minted directly from self-custody.

Our depositors "self-wrap" by locking BTC with themselves. They send BTC to a special type of multisig called a DLC that can only pay out to them.

DLCs were invented at MIT by the creator of the Lightning Network, and were added in Taproot
