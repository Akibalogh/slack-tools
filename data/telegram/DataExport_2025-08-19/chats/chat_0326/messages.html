Aki Balogh
Hi @icefreshmilk ! We're be very happy to participate in this with you.

There's only one difference, which I'm trying to reconcile. We are not a blockchain, and we don't have a token that would get taken as gas fees. Retail cannot mint dlcBTC — only institutions can. So, we can give dlcBTC merchants points for putting in their BTC.. but they don't need to accomplish tasks to do this. They just need to qualify and sign our MSA (Master Services Agreement).

Retail can interface with us in two ways. First, they can swap wBTC-dlcBTC in our Curve pool. Second, we are launching a $DLC token in Q3, which is an oracle network that lets node operators (our DLC Attestors) to stake $DLC and earn rewards in $DLC. Retail can buy $DLC and delegate to attestors.

So, in our system, we could give "points" to our merchants, but we cannot give retail points for swapping. We could give retail points for buying $DLC, but that seems redundant.

And, since we're not a chain, we don't have developers who want to build on DLC.Link.
