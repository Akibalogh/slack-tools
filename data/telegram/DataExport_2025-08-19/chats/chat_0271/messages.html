qBTC – Bitcoin’s Post-Quantum Continuity Layer

What it is
* One-to-one SHA-256 proof-of-work sidechain; Bitcoin holders can move coins today and transact securely after ECDSA is obsolete.
* Consensus parameters mirror Bitcoin (10-minute blocks, equal throughput); miners redeploy existing hardware and preserve hash-rate economics.
* Pre-Q-day workflow maps legacy ECDSA UTXOs to post-quantum keys without on-chain key exposure. Users secure future control without bridging coins now.

Why it matters
* Seamless quantum upgrade path: no changes to Bitcoin mainnet required.
* Eliminates governance-latency risk; holders act immediately instead of waiting for BIP adoption.
* Preserves Bitcoin’s monetary policy, governance model, and self-custody principles.
* Neutralizes “Bitcoin-has-no-plan” narrative and dampens confidence-shock risk.
* Institutional Bitcoin investors need to know that there is a credible plan in place should Q-day happen before full BIP upgrades

Where we are
* Open-source client available for public review.
* qBTC team co-authored draft BIP with core developer Jameson Lopp.
* Devnet live → Public Testnet July 2025 → Mainnet October 2025.
* DNA Fund seed completed (Q1 2025). Current raise: $5 M at $75 M post-money
