I am sorry for the incovenience! We had a lot of troubles with ledger, most of them caused by their behaviour on the starknet app.
To give you some context, I only saw what you are experiencing in this flow:

1. You try to do a transaction, but ledger is not connected, or not on starknet app, or sleeping
2. We call get public key but ledger fails and it caches the error
3. Even if you connect now the ledger and open starknet, it will continue to fail

They will have some releases that will improve this, and we will also have some on our side.

That's why I think if you reinstall + connect ledger again and make sure it's on starknet app and awake it should work
