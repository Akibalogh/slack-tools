Biturbo is a new hybrid blockchain that merges the best parts of Bitcoin and Ethereum, creating a more inclusive and dynamic ecosystem. This approach is a significant step forward in blockchain design.

Biturbo rewards active participation and liquidity provision by using Proof of Transaction (PoT) and Proof of Liquidity (PoL). These consensus mechanisms ensure transaction activity and secure networks, providing a superior and safe user experience.
