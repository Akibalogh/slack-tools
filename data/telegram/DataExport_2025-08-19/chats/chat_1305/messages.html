Baptiste | Merkl
Hey @akibalogh , great to meet you as well!

In short, Merkl is a managed incentives system for various defi assets, specialized on concentrated liquidity pool.

The way it works is very simple:
1. You set a start date, an end date and an amount of tokens
2. You select a pool and configure how you want to reward users (based on fees or TVL)
3. You create the campaign

Merkl will then linearly distribute the tokens to ALL the LPs of the pool. Merkl natively handles reward forwarding to active liquidity managers such as Arrakis, Gamma or Beefy (we support over 25 ALMs). This means that if you create a campaign on a UNiV3 pool, both direct LPs (who hold the position NFT directly) and indirect LPs (people who provide liquidity through ALMs) will be rewarded.

The main advantage compared to Arrakis the following:
- If you incentivize via Arrakis, you will only reward Arrakis LPs (whales are not big fans of ALMs) and you will reward the LPs even if the position held by the Arrakis vault is out of range.
- With Merkl, you're litterally going to make all LPs and ALMs compete with each other to get the rewards, this result in much more efficient incentives spend (very tight liquidity around the range for example)
- The Merkl app has 100k unique users every month and has built in reporting for your campaigns (enabling you to track what users received rewards etc)
