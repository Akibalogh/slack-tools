Two comments on the doc:

1. Our marketing team came up with the term "bridgeless", but we shouldn't take that literally. Ours is not a simple MPC bridge, but it does use a network of oracles (attestors) to wrap BTC to other chains. For accuracy, especially for this audience, we could just call it a "safer bridge"

2. In the About block, let's not mention Discreet Log Contracts. We don't use DLCs anymore - we moved to PSBTs approx. 6 months ago. Our tech still is unique in that it uses "self-wrapping", but I'd like to be precise. We've also rebranded from dlcBTC to iBTC to reflect this.
