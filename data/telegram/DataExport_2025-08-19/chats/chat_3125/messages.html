Our customer types:

1. Proprietary Trading Desks (Prop Shops): Firms that use their own capital to trade for profit. They don't have clients and take on full risk of their trades.

2. Hedge Funds: Investment funds that use various strategies to earn returns for their investors. They often use leverage and derivatives to enhance returns and manage risk.

3. Investment Bank Trading Desks: These desks trade on behalf of clients or the bank itself. They engage in a wide range of activities, including market making, underwriting, and managing client portfolios.

4. Algorithmic Trading Firms: Use mathematical models and algorithms to make high-speed trades based on market data.

5. High-Frequency Trading (HFT) Firms: A subset of algorithmic trading, these firms use sophisticated algorithms to trade at extremely high speeds.

6. Commodity Trading Desks: Specialize in trading physical commodities like oil, metals, or agricultural products, or their derivatives.

7. Forex Desks: Focus on trading currencies in the foreign exchange market.

8. Quantitative Trading Desks: Use quantitative analysis to make trading decisions. This can overlap with algorithmic and HFT firms.

Other customer profiles:

* OTC Desks

* Lending Desks
