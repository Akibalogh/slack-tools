Hi, sorry for not responding, had quite busy weeks. I went through the document. I think it is a good solution for a limited number of verified merchants. As I heard from Aki, it is your option - merchants will be custody solutions, so you will have tens instead of thousands. Having a large number of merchants makes it harder from my perspective:
- Depending on how you are going to build the redeem script, the number of merchants will be technically limited or transaction fees will be higher.
- With a large number of merchants managing UTXO is harder, both when adding new ones and removing compromised ones.

I have a few tech questions:
1. How are you going to implement the redeem script that handles merchant signatures: taproot, OP_CHECKMULTISIG, some signature aggregation?
2. How are attestor network participants incentivized to work?
