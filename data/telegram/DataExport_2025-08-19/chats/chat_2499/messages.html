Hey Aki,

Thanks a ton for your time earlier this week. It's been a pleasure getting to know you and chatting about dlcBTC.

In the interest of being respectful of your time: I was able to sync internally and our decision is to not pursue the current financing.

In case the feedback is helpful to you: the main reasons are twofold:

1. We fear that it's really hard to monetize wrapped/bridged Bitcoin. The fees generated by wBTC, even at their scale, are not particularly attractive. To some extent we almost see the product as potential loss leader.

2. Our perception is that the revealed preference so far has been to go towards trusted custodians, and we feel this trend will likely continue. We believe that companies better equipped to compete in this space are probably ones with large existing distribution and trusted brands as custodians (e.g., Coinbase or other large exchanges).

That said, it's immediately clear that the approach you're taking is extremely well thought out. We appreciate that you're really pushing the envelope on an interesting technical solution, and we hope to see it succeed!

If you're open to it, we'd love to stay in touch.
