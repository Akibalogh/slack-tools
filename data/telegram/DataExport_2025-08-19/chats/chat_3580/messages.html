Aki Balogh
Hi Pazd! Pleasure talking through this. To summarize in notes:

DLCs represent the only way to enable trustless betting with native BTC. Any other method uses wrapping or bridging which introduces custodial risks, including the risk of regulation
13:58
There are three components to it:
* A BTC wallet that supports DLC signing. Currently we're building & launching this in XVerse.app. We can build into other BTC wallets also
* A liquidator that can hold BTC and trade it for wBTC
* A pool on your end that handles the native Bitcoin bets
13:58
We're in alpha stage, so we're still figuring out the best implementation models. Right now, we can represent escrowed BTC as an NFT on the other chain. It cannot be an ERC-20 because the escrow itself is locked & non-fungible.

One potential way around this could be to implement on your side using a pool of ERC-1155 tokens. These can be minted and moved around on your side. When the deposit token is burned, the BTC is returned. I've looped in my co-founder Jesse (our CTO) to comment
