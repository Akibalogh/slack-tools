Overview

Although Bitcoin is the largest digital asset, it cannot be used in DeFi without first transferring to a custodian or a bridge. However, counterparty risks and bridge hacks have led to over $140Bn in losses.

DLC.Link utilizes Discreet Log Contracts, invented at MIT by the co-creator of the Lightning Network, to provide a theft-proof bridge to DeFi on Ethereum. 

We've recently launched dlcBTC, a safer wrapped Bitcoin. dlcBTC is the first native wrapped Bitcoin on Arbitrum, Base and XRPL.

We're raised $3Mn to date from OKX, ABCDE, Foresight, Chainlink, Sequoia Scout and 15 other funds, and are raising $5Mn this summer to expand sales & marketing.

Investor Deck: https://bit.ly/DLC-Link-Investor-Presentation


Seed round terms (as of 7/29/24):
We're raising $5Mn USD, with the potential to oversubscribe.

To date, we've received $800k in hard commits from: Round 13, Monday Capital, Paul Taylor (BlackRock) and Mayur Gupta (CMO of Kraken).

Seed round investors can invest in one of two ways:
1. Invest USD on a SAFE + Token warrant at $100Mn post-money, with no liquidity provision.
2. Invest USD on a SAFE + Token warrant at $70Mn post-money, with a liquidity provision clause.

Liquidity provision: the investor or their affiliate agrees to become a dlcBTC Merchant. Merchant agrees to self-wrap 1 BTC for each $10k invested, until dlcBTC exceeds $150Mn TVL.
